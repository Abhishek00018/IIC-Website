Who we are?

The club is furnished with an incubation center,where thoughts can be transformed into reality with the support of our wise and expert advisors. We at IIC motivate students towards Entrepreneurship, and help them to convert their Ideas into Products. With this, we focus to provide budding techpreneurs an ecosystem of collaborative mentorship and professional networking.

Our Mission.

Innovation Club is envisaged to network people, ideas, experiences and resources to galvanize the innovation community in LNMIIT. This club not only helps to nurture your ideas and encourage you to bring up business proposals but there is also an Incubation centre where in after rigorous evaluation of your idea the institute may provide you with the required facilities and perhaps you can even get funds for your project.
